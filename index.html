About Us

Girnar Shilp is a family-owned business with a legacy of over 80 years in crafting exquisite marble masterpieces. Our commitment to excellence is deeply rooted in the passion and dedication of the Khetani family, with our founder, Arvindbhai Khetani, and co-founder, Sureshbhai Khetani. Today, Yash Khetani leads as the CEO, carrying on our tradition of excellence.

What sets us apart is our relentless pursuit of precision and craftsmanship. We take immense pride in our ability to transform raw marble into stunning note planks, intricate temples, and lifelike statues that encapsulate our rich heritage and artistic expertise.

With eight decades of experience, we've achieved remarkable milestones and forged enduring partnerships with our clients. We're here to bring your dreams to life through our exquisite marble creations, each piece bearing the hallmark of Girnar Shilp's unmatched artistry.

Meet the talented individuals who make up the heart of Girnar Shilp. Our team is dedicated to providing you with unparalleled artistry and ensuring your complete satisfaction.
